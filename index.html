WELCOME TO ELECTION COMMISSION
IIT KANPUR

The election commission is mandated to foster the democratic spirit in the students’ self-governance set up - the Students’ Gymkhana.
For upholding this spirit, it is necessary that the representatives of the students are elected by the process of
free and fair elections.
